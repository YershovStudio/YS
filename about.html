My name is Oleg Yershov, a designer & photographer based in Lowell. I run Yershov Studio as a space to show my photography, design, and image-based storytelling.

My work centers on portraiture, with an emphasis on lighting and controlled setups, while also extending into more casual, observational photography. I strive to create bold, high-impact work that uses drama, colors, and composition as a visual tool in both design and photography.

I treat portraiture as a constructed process, using lighting and creative effects to shape images that feel intentional and dramatic. I do indoor & outdoor photoshoots, group photos, and graduation work, focusing on creating images that feel personal, elevated, and unique.
